## Research Interests

I am passionate about interdisciplinary research at the frontier of AI, particularly the translation of transformative techniques to **biopharmaceutical** and **healthcare** development. I aspire to contribute to *fair, accessible, and trustworthy* solutions to real-world problems. These interests have drawn me to explore three main perspectives:

- **Foundation Models for Scientific Discovery.** Especially in life sciences — how can we build effective foundation models that grasp the *upstream* structure of heterogeneous, high-dimensional, long-context biological data, and that drive genuine discoveries in biology and health?
- **Data Quality, Privacy & Fairness.** Subject to the core constraints of biological privacy and diversity, how do we apply more advanced data-processing methods to guarantee access to high-quality data, mitigating bias and imbalance — so that the corpora can soundly support model evaluation, iteration, and generalization?
- **Applications Bridging Models and Data.** How can we more effectively uncover correclations between new drugs (and their targets), or specific human conditions, and the multi-modal data collected and processed through AI techniques? And how to ensure that these applications are made fairly and democratically accessible?
